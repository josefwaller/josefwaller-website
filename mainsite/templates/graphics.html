A platformer I made in Python using Pygame. You can download it here or view the source on Github.

Play as a knight with no regard to anything but his own amusement, in a world intentionally made to look bad. Use a very limited array of tools and enjoy generic, unimaginative platforming in a harshly unforgiving and unfair environment only to kill everybody at the end.

If you find a bug, or want to make a suggestion, please let me know at josef@josefwaller.com.
